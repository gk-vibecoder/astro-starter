Before Acme, our release cycle was chaos. We had spreadsheets tracking spreadsheets, and Slack threads that nobody could find.

## The challenge

BuildCo had grown from 5 to 40 engineers in 18 months. Our old processes couldn't scale. We were shipping bugs faster than features.

## What changed

We started with Aria AI's anomaly detection on our deploy pipeline. Within the first week, it flagged three recurring failure patterns we'd been working around for months.

> "Aria found patterns in our data we hadn't noticed in two years. That insight alone was worth it."

The second win was automated triage. Instead of a senior engineer spending 2 hours diagnosing each incident, Aria would surface the most likely cause with 85% accuracy.

## The result

- Release cycle: 3 weeks → 5 days
- Incident MTTR: 4 hours → 45 minutes
- Engineer satisfaction score: +32 points (internal survey)

If you're scaling an engineering team and still doing triage manually, Acme is the first thing I'd add.
